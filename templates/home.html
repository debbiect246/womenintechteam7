About

Here at Pedicoders we believe women are unstoppable.
Identifying as a woman brings a new and innovative perspective to a currently male dominated industry.
Despite common misconceptions, women make excellent coders. In fact, there is some suggestion women might even be better coders than men *gasp*. Previous research has shown that code written by women was viewed more favourably when viewers didn't know it was written by a woman.
But times have changed.
Coding is more accessible than ever and we want everyone talking about it. So pull up a chair with your friends, mothers, daughters and everyone in between and get coding. Whether you're a complete beginner, or seasoned professional, we want to hear from you all.

Meetups

Women who code together grow together, and we want to bring people together.
We want everyone to feel included and encouraged, so we host a whole range of events to suit you.
From study sessions to get help and insight into your work, to coffee dates to take a break and have some banter with like minded people.
So don't be shy. Come along to an event near you, or organise your own. There are no limits to what you can do.
